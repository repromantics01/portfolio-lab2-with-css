About Me

A passionate learner who is always seeking to improve.

Hi, I’m Pauline! I’m a web development enthusiast and computer science student with a growing passion for building websites that are not just functional but also enjoyable to use. While I’m still learning and improving every day, I’ve gained some experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and a bit of UI/UX design along the way.

I’ve worked on a few projects that have helped me better understand the process of turning ideas into real, working websites. From small personal projects to team collaborations, every experience has taught me something valuable about coding, problem-solving, and even design.

I’m always eager to learn more and take on new challenges, whether that’s improving my coding skills or diving deeper into database management and software engineering concepts. I believe there’s always room for growth, and I approach every task with curiosity and determination.

When I’m not working on web development, you’ll often find me writing, drawing, or exploring new ways to combine creativity and technology. I may not have all the answers yet, but I’m excited about the journey ahead and the opportunities to continue growing in this field.

If you’re looking for someone who’s hardworking, eager to learn, and always open to feedback, I’d love to connect!
